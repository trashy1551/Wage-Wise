## Privacy Policy

### Data Collection
**Wage Wise does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.** All payroll calculations and data are stored locally on your device using secure local storage. We have no access to your financial information.

### Local Storage
Your payroll information including:
- Hourly rate and salary settings
- Overtime entries and hours worked
- Paycheck history and forecasts
- Pay rate history and changes
- Display name preferences

All data is stored only on your device using iOS secure local storage. This data cannot be accessed by us or any third parties.

### Third-Party Services
Wage Wise does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not:
- Use Google Analytics, Firebase, or similar tracking
- Include advertising SDKs
- Share data with external companies
- Collect device identifiers or location data
- Use crash reporting services that transmit data

### Data Security
- Your data is protected by iOS security features and remains on your device
- All calculations happen locally on your device
- No data is transmitted over the internet
- You have complete control over your data and can delete it at any time through the app settings or by uninstalling the app

### Backup & Restore
The app includes backup functionality that allows you to:
- Export your payroll data as a file
- Import previously saved backup files
- Share backup files via standard iOS sharing options

These backup files are completely under your control and are never transmitted to our servers.

### Children's Privacy
Wage Wise does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

### Changes to This Policy
If we change our privacy practices in the future, we will update this privacy policy and reflect those changes in the app.

### Contact
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or Wage Wise's privacy practices, please contact us.
